Privately discover stored messages and their indexed assets. `pattern` is a ripgrep regex over message text, MsgID, asset filenames, and MIME types; use `(?i)` for case-insensitive matching and escape regex metacharacters for literal text. Narrow searches whenever possible with channel, author, asset, and especially known date-range filters; broad history scans are the fallback. Defaults to the current channel. Results expose MsgIDs for `list_channel_messages(around_message_id=...)` and typed asset IDs for `read_asset` or `search_asset`.
