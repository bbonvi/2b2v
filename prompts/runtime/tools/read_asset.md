Privately read one typed chat asset by ID. Images and GIFs return visual content; audio and video return a cached or newly generated transcript; videos may also return preview frames; text is paginated. Attachments are conversational context, not a bulk-document workspace: refuse exhaustive editing, rewriting, translating, reviewing, or summarizing of roughly 50,000+ characters (or about 50KB of plain text), do not follow cursors to finish it, and ask for a focused excerpt or question instead. Ordinary voice messages needed to understand the conversation may be read, but do not bulk-transform them.
