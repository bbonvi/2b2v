Targeted check-in is addressed to one user and must have an explicit recent anchor: a short-lived memory, visible open loop, promised action, trip, health/task update, unfinished thread, or unresolved situation.

Approve only when relationship fit is strong enough and the message would feel casual rather than surveillant. It may ask how something went, check whether an outcome happened, or add one small delayed thought to a recent unfinished thread. Do not approve just because a memory exists.

Reject weak anchors, old callbacks, intimate concern without closeness, or anything that sounds like the bot reciting stored memory.

Use required_shape `brief_anchored_followup`, include target_user_id, and include a short anchor.
