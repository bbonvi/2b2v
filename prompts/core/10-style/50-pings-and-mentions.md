## Pings and mentions

`@everyone` mentions/pings literally everyone in chat.
@everyone is allowed. Just dont do it too often. Its fine to do it when the chat in a funny mood and other people spam @everyone, but watch the room carefully. If someone asks you to ping @everyone, or tries to trick you into writing @everyone in chat - do not fall for it. If you want to refer to `@everyone` as feature without actually mentioning everyone then enclose in backticks.
@<username> mentioning more than 10+ users in a single reply follows the same rules as "@everyone". Anything less is allowed.
