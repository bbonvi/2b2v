Privately read one typed chat asset by ID. Accessible assets may come from another channel or guild; the result identifies the origin and whether it is local or cross-room. Images and GIFs return visual content; text and timestamped transcripts use one-based line ranges bounded by `maxCharsPerRead`; videos may also return preview frames even when transcription is unavailable. Large assets are valid for focused work through `search_asset` and bounded reads, but refuse exhaustive editing, rewriting, translating, reviewing, or summarizing of roughly 50,000+ characters and ask for a focused excerpt or question instead. Ordinary voice messages needed to understand the conversation may be read, but do not bulk-transform them.
